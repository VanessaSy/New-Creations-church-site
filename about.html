Our Story

New Creations Church was founded in 2012 with a vision to bring transformation to the community through the teachings of Jesus Christ. What began as a small fellowship of believers has grown into a vibrant, multi-generational church committed to serving God and loving people.

Our Mission

To spread the gospel and the love of Christ to everyone in the community. We believe in empowering believers to grow in faith, serve with compassion, and lead lives that reflect Christ's love.

Our Vision

To be a Christ-centered church where lives are transformed, families are restored, and communities are strengthened by the power of the Holy Spirit.
